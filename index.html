About us

Transforming Complexity into Simplicity!

Ours is a team of computer and networking gurus that aim at making effortless connections by powering secure and scalable networks, all the time. A Company built on a shared passion for innovation by Majid and Alforce delivering cutting-edge solutions. .

FORJITECK

Forjiteck is a Malawian-based Internet Service Provider (ISP) dedicated to delivering fast, reliable and affordable internet connectivity to individuals, businesses and organizations.

Our Mission

To bridge the digital divide in Malawi by providing high-quality internet services that empower communities, foster economic growth and improve quality of life.

Our Services

We offer a range of internet plans, including: Fiber-optic connections, Wireless connections and Satellite connections. Our plans are designed to meet the unique needs of our customers, ensuring that everyone stays connected..

FAQ

What Inspired the Co-Founders to Start Forjitek?

The co-founders of Forjitek were inspired by a shared passion for technology and innovation. Seeing the growing need for reliable IT solutions, they decided to create a company that offers modern networking infrastructure, software development and AI solutions to businesses and individuals, helping them stay ahead in the digital world..

+

How Do I Get Started with Forjitek?

Getting started is easy! Simply reach out to us through our website or contact us directly. We’ll schedule a consultation to discuss your needs and provide a customized solution for your business.

+

When Should I Contact Forjitek for Support?

You can contact Forjitek anytime you need assistance with your IT infrastructure. Whether you’re setting up a new system, facing technical issues, or looking to upgrade your existing services, our team is ready to assist you.

+

Can Forjitek Help Upgrade My Existing IT Infrastructure?

Yes! We specialize in evaluating current IT setups and offering recommendations for upgrades. Whether it's improving your network security, enhancing your website, or introducing new software, we ensure that your IT infrastructure remains competitive.

+

How Can Forjitek Help My Business Grow?

Forjitek helps businesses grow by providing tailored IT solutions that improve efficiency, security and customer engagement. Our networking services ensure seamless communication, while our software and AI solutions help automate tasks and provide data-driven insights.
